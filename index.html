Welcome to cbLinux

The world's first linux distro built for crytobanking!

In a world where a computer holds all our money, we need a safe environment to store your money and conduct transactions. cbLinux was designed from the ground up to be safe from hackers and secure with encryption. Its core is the time-tested Linux operating system, Ubuntu specifically for its ease of use. On top of the rock solid Linux core, the lightweight xfce desktop environment provides a clean and usable interface for both beginners and experts. Finally, cbLinux is prepackaged with your favorite software like truecrypt, electrum, bitcoin-qt, otr messaging, and thunderbird.

Your OS is not is not ready for cryptocurrency:

Isolation

On your regular OS, you have lots of programs that all share the same file space. Well, suppose one gets a little gready and wants to start touching your online wallet. With cbLinux, that doesn't happen. Only open source programs, bare-minimum programs are included. Using cbLinux, you can rest assured that stray programs won't be making a run with your wallet.

Privacy

Upon booting into cbLinux, you are immediatly connected to the tor network, a secure network in which information is encrypted and anonymized. Your ISP and anyone else who takes an interest in you (the NSA) can snoop on the normal traffic originating from your original OS.

Encryption

Truecrypt, a community vetted, open source encryption tool is included to provide military grade disk encryption for the all senstive files you don't want falling into the wrong hands.

Open Source

Every line of code in cbLinux is open source. That means you or anyone else can read it line by line and personally vet it. There are no suprises here.
