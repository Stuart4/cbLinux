Welcome to cbLinux

The world's first linux distro built for crytobanking!

In a world where a computer holds all our money, we need a safe environment to store your money and conduct transactions. cbLinux was designed from the ground up to be safe from hackers and secure with encryption. Its core is the time-tested Linux operating system, Ubuntu specifically for its ease of use. On top of the rock solid Linux core, the lightweight xfce desktop environment provides a clean and usable interface for both beginners and experts.
